About Me

Engineering ML & AI systems for the enterprise

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer with 19 years of overall software engineering experience, the last 5+ years focused on Machine Learning, Generative AI, and MLOps. I specialize in the end-to-end ML lifecycle — from pipeline integration and design reviews to production deployment and operational support in enterprise-grade environments.

My recent work centers on architecting and deploying large language model (LLM) solutions in production, including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, agentic AI frameworks, fine-tuned domain-specific models, LLM-as-judge evaluation rubrics, and inference served through Amazon Bedrock.

I have extensive experience designing and maintaining end-to-end ML pipelines (MLOps) — data ingestion, model training, model serving, monitoring, and automated retraining — along with strong expertise in Bayesian linear regression for financial domain use cases, and model interpretability with SHAP and LIME for transparent, auditable systems.
